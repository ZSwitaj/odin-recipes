Nachos


Nachos are another great hot snack with endless possibilites. Anything but olives, f'n gross!

Ingredients

  • Totilla Chips
  • Cheese
  • Salsa
  • Peppers
  • Onions
  • Pepperoni
  • Spinach
  • Jalapenos

Steps

  1. Distribute the chips evenly on a baking sheet
  2. Sprinkle the cheese on. Nice and heavy!
  3. Sprinkle on the toppings
  4. Bake at 350 degrees until cheese is melty
